Student capstone project

Foundation Sixty6

Working in small groups and functioning as a mini-studio, level 4 students will design, develop, and pitch a professional marketing and advertising campaign.

The mission of Foundation Sixty6 is to facilitate improved access to mental healthcare for youth between the ages of 16 and 25 suffering from psychosis, bipolar disorder (BPD), and schizophrenia, specifically within Southwestern Ontario.
